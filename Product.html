In collaboration with renowned Cape Town artist, Shaune Rogatschnig. Set of four (4) Food Covers. The most beautiful solution to go green in your home. Our handmade covers are locally manufactured from 100%, high quality organic cotton.

These covers are a great alternative to cling wrap and can be used to keep food fresh and keep bugs and flies away. They're pretty enough to be used at any dinner table or at the outside braai.

The 100% cotton fabric allows for air and steam to escape. All fabrics have been pre-shrunk and are machine washable. Bright colours may run, so wash separately. Covers are hand stitched and finished with elastic on the edges to ensure a snug fit around various container sizes.

Our Food Cover Artist Range (set of 4) includes:

Pickle (XS): The baby of the food covers. Teeny weeny. Can fit bowl size up to 14 cm.

Dip (S): Small but versatile. Can fit bowl size up to 20 cm.

Snack (M): Perfect for in between sizes. Can fit bowl size up to 25 cm.

Salad (L): Can fit bowl size up to 36 cm.

Shaune Rogatschnig is a highly regarded artist from Cape Town, South Africa. Her current artwork are bold representations of indigenous plant and fynbos landscapes of South Africa. Her work is dominated by her love for indigenous flora and the way each bloom captures light.
